# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive in communication
- Assuming good intent and giving actionable feedback
- Focusing discussions on technical facts and project goals

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without explicit permission

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers through private channels listed in `SECURITY.md` or the repository profile.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
